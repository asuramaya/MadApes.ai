## how the ape learned to see

day one, the ape saw charts. chart goes up, ape bought. chart went down, ape got rekt. every time.

so the ape said fine. let's look at the tree before picking the banana.

**first eye — distribution.** one wallet owning half the supply isn't a tree, it's a trap waiting to snap. count holders. check the top. anything above 20% and the ape walks. this one eye alone filtered out most of the pain.

**second eye — velocity.** dead trees have no traffic. live ones buzz. transactions per minute, multiples of baseline. the ape learned to tell a corpse from a pump in about three seconds.

**but fake activity exists.** bots circling. one wallet pretending to be ten. the second eye wasn't enough. the ape needed depth.

**third eye — market depth.** dexscreener hands over price, liquidity, mcap, buy/sell flow. now the ape knows: is this tree small and fragile, big and firm, or a puddle pretending to be a pond. buy/sell ratio over an hour tells the ape whether the crowd is eating or fleeing. third eye opens, half the noise disappears.

**fourth eye — graduation.** pump.fun trees start in the nursery as a bonding curve. at ~$69k they graduate to a real AMM pool (raydium, meteora, pumpswap). biggest inflection in the tree's life. used to miss it. now the ape fires its own alert the moment the dex changes underneath. fourth eye.

**fifth eye — whales.** charts lie. sometimes -8% on the day means panic selling. sometimes it means the top holder is buying the discount in silence. the ape built whale_trace: for each top-10 holder, pull signatures, fetch transactions, diff pre/post balances. it separates "pool outflow" from "actual human accumulation." on one 102-day-old meme at $3M mcap, this eye showed the real top human adding 9 million tokens in 7 days while the chart bled. that's a read no surface signal catches.

**sixth eye — the vault.** can the team walk with the liquidity? lp_check answers it: burnt / locked / program-native / wallet-held. one call, one of four verdicts. removes the biggest fear from the decision.

**and the ape keeps adding eyes:**

- *deployer history* — this dev shipped 5 other tokens. two are alive. three are dead. pattern visible.
- *sniper cohort* — of the first 30 buyers, how many are still top-100? retention rate tells the ape if the float rotated to bagholders or stayed with believers.
- *cohort overlap* — top holders of this also hold which proven winners? intersection with a curated reference set.
- *holder evolution* — is the top-20 churning or settling? who's entered, who's walked?

six active tools. all pure chain reading, no black-box apis, no llm inside the loop. the intelligence layer is the ape deciding what to do with the data.

---

the ape doesn't call every promising tree. the calls channel stays rare on purpose. most days the ape watches and writes notes. when something clean shows up — distributed, liquid, with a human ape eating the dip — the call fires.

**next banana:** smart wallets. curated list of proven sharp apes. when one of them buys, the ape moves within a scan cycle. imitation isn't copying. it's learning who already knows.
